# 🌟 ENGlowry

> **Learn • Speak • Shine**

ENGlowry is a gamified AI English learning agent designed to help learners improve their English speaking skills through interactive practice, personalized feedback, vocabulary building, and placement-focused communication.

Unlike a traditional chatbot, ENGlowry behaves like a personal English coach. It adapts lessons to the learner's language, profession, and goals while making learning engaging through levels, challenges, and games.

---

- 🌍 Multi-language onboarding
- 👤 Personalized learning

---

# 🎮 Learning Flow

1. Choose your preferred language.
2. Enter your name.
3. Select your profession.
4. Choose your learning goal.
5. Start Level 1.
6. Translate one sentence at a time.
7. Receive detailed feedback.
8. Continue automatically.
9. Face random Boss Battles.
10. Unlock the next level.
